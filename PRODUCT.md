## What We Are NOT

- NOT a classifieds website
- NOT a marketplace for selling
- NOT a competitor to Sahibinden or Letgo
- NOT a simple ads aggregator

---

## What We Are

**2ElBul is the Market Intelligence Platform for the Turkish Second-Hand Market.**

We aggregate, normalize, and analyze second-hand market data to provide:
- Reliable market insights
- Price intelligence
- Category understanding
- Search intelligence
- Duplicate detection
- Confidence scoring

---

## Core Vision

In 5 years, 2ElBul should be the authoritative source for second-hand market data in Turkey.

Users should come to 2ElBul to understand market trends, not to buy or sell.

---

## Mission

Provide transparent, reliable, and actionable market intelligence for the Turkish second-hand economy.

## Decision Framework

Before any feature, ask:

1. **Does it improve market intelligence?**
2. **Does it maintain data quality?**
3. **Is it professional and trusted?**
4. **Is it research-focused?**
5. **Would a market analyst want this?**

If NO to any question: Don't build it.
